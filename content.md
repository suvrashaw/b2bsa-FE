## **QUALIFICATION FRAMEWORKS  ← Move earlier per PDF correction**

### **H2: How We Qualify Leads — BANT and CHAMP**

We use two structured qualification frameworks, agreed with your sales team before the event begins:

BANT — Budget (is there budget available?), Authority (is this the decision-maker?), Need (is there a verified business problem?), Timeline (when are they solving it?). Ideal for enterprise sales with clear procurement cycles.

**CHAMP** — Challenges (what is the core problem?), Authority (who owns the decision?), Money (is budget allocated?), Prioritisation (how urgent is this?). Ideal for consultative, complex sales where the challenge comes first.

Both frameworks ensure qualification happens during the live **booth conversation** — not retrospectively from a contact list.
